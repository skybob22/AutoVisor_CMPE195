About AutoVisor

AutoVisor is a web based application that has automated the advising system that benefits both advisors and students by recommending appropriate courses to students based on their preferences and major requirements. The application parses through a student's past courses, academic performance, graduation requirements, course prerequisites, and availability to suggest what classes the student might be interested in going forward. The primary purpose of this application is to streamline the process of getting immediate, personalized advising for their major and to remove advising holds in a matter of seconds.
